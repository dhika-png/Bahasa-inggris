Text Type

👤 Biography Text

What is Biography Text?

A biography text is a detailed description of a person's life written by someone else. It tells about the person's birth, education, career, achievements, and significant events. When a person writes about their own life, it is called an autobiography.

Generic Structure

  1. Orientation — Introduces the person: name, birth date, birthplace, family background.
  2. Events / Life Story — Important events in chronological order: education, career, achievements, struggles.
  3. Re-orientation — Closing about the person's legacy or current status.

Language Features

  • Uses simple past tense and present perfect tense
  • Uses specific dates and places (e.g., born on July 12, 1920)
  • Uses action verbs (e.g., studied, founded, won)
  • Uses third person pronouns (he, she, his, her)
  • Uses chronological connectors (e.g., in 1990, later, finally)
"A biography is more than just facts — it tells the story of a person's journey, their struggles, triumphs, and the impact they made on the world."

📌 Example:

"Marie Curie was born on November 7, 1867, in Warsaw, Poland. She was the youngest of five children and showed exceptional intelligence from an early age."
